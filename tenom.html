TENOM

Tenom is the native token of the MONET Network

Tenom token will be used as:

1. A mean of transacting on ad hoc blockchains and MONET Hub

2. As a reward to validators for securing MONET Hub

3. And to participate in MONET Hub governance and to determine the weight of an individual validator decisions in the voting (staking)

Regulatory

Tenom is a payment token approved by the Swiss Financial regulators FINMA
